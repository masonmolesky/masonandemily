What time should I arrive?

Doors will open at 3:30, at which point you will be able to drop your coat and find a seat. We will begin promptly at 4:00 pm.

Will the wedding be live-streamed?

No, unfortunately we will not be offering live streaming of the event.

Can I bring my kids to the wedding?

Yes! We want our wedding to be a family friendly event and ask that you note your RSBP any kids that will be coming along to the wedding. However, we do ask that for kids younger than 16, that they do not attend the ceremony nor reception. Instead, we will be providing babysitting and an alternative space/games/food. To ensure we have adequate babysitters, please make sure you kids under babysitting in your RSVP.

Where can I find my kids? Who will be babysitting them?

We will be providing more information to parents closer to the event so watch out for another email from us. However, Kids will be located in the basement of the venue where they will have food, activities, and babysitters.

May I bring a date?

Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. If you received a plus one they will appear under your name when you RSVP.

Is there a dress code?

We'd love to see our family and friends dress up with us! We are requesting formal attire for our evening. We encourage everyone to wear Christmas colors - red and green - and ask that ladies please avoid white dresses.

How will dinner be served?

We will be serving a plated meal with a choice of salad (house or caesar) and main (chicken, fish, or beef). Vegan options also provided. Please note any further dietary restrictions in your RSVP and we will make sure those are passed along to the chef.

Is there transportation or shuttles to and from the hotels and wedding venue?

Yes! We will be providing shuttles both to and from the wedding venue from the hotels. Shuttles will leave periodically throughout the night from the venue back to the hotels. More details on specifics will be provided closer to the wedding day.
