What time should I arrive?

Doors will open at 3:30 PM, giving you time to drop your coat and find your seat. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00 PM.

Will the wedding be live-streamed?

No, unfortunately, we will not be offering live streaming of the event.

Can I bring my kids to the wedding?

Yes! We want our wedding to be family-friendly. For children under 16, we kindly ask that they do not attend the ceremony or reception. Instead, we will provide babysitting services and an alternative space with games and food. To ensure we have enough babysitters, please include your children’s details in the babysitting section of your RSVP.

Where can I find my kids? Who will be babysitting them?

We’ll share more information to parents closer to the event via email. Children will be cared for by babysitters in the venue’s basement, where they’ll have access to food and activities.

May I bring a date?

Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. If you received a plus one they will appear under your name when you RSVP.

Is there a dress code?

We’d love to see everyone dress up for our special day! We’re requesting formal attire, and we encourage guests to incorporate Christmas colors — red and green — into your outfits.

How will dinner be served?

Dinner will be a plated meal with the following options:

  • Salad: House or Caesar
  • Main: Chicken, fish, or beef (vegan options available)
    • If you have additional dietary restrictions, please note them in your RSVP, and we’ll make sure they’re accommodated.

    Is there transportation or shuttles to and from the hotels and wedding venue?

    Yes! We’ll provide shuttles to and from the wedding venue for guests staying at the listed hotels. Shuttles will run periodically throughout the evening. More specific details will be shared as we get closer to the big day.
