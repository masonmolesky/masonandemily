Travel By Car


From Washington, D.C., the drive takes about 1 hour 30 minutes, depending on traffic.

  • Take I-66 West toward Front Royal
  • Take Exit 6 for US-340/US-522 toward Front Royal
  • Turn right onto US-522 North / Winchester Road
  • The wedding venue entrance is located on the left 6 miles north and the wedding hotel is located 2 miles north on the left.

    • Ample parking is available directly outside the venue.










    Travel by Air


    The two closest major airports are:

  • Dulles International Airport (IAD): About 1 hour west of Washington, D.C. and approximately 1 hour from the venue by car.
  • Reagan National Airport (DCA): Roughly 1 hour and 30 minutes from the venue, depending on traffic.

    • Both airports offer a variety of rental car and rideshare options.

    Travel By Bus


    If you'd rather not drive, one bus lines offer regular service to Front Royal from both Union Station (D.C.'s train station) and Dulles Airport (IAD):

  • MegaBus/Virginia Breeze: From Union Station, leaving every day at 9:35 AM, the trip takes about 2 hours and 10 minutes.
  • MegaBus/Virginia Breeze: From Dulles Airport, leaving every day at 10:45 AM, the trip takes about 1 hour.
